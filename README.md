# Russia – Ukraine War Impact on India Dashboard

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes the economic and trade impact of the Russia–Ukraine war on India. The project provides insights into inflation trends, commodity price fluctuations, trade balance, imports, and sector-wise economic performance during different phases of the war.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze the impact of the Russia–Ukraine conflict on India's economy.
- Monitor changes in crude oil, fertilizer, and wheat prices.
- Compare import trends from Russia and Ukraine.
- Evaluate inflation and GDP growth across sectors.
- Study regional trade balance and exchange rate variations.
- Visualize trade value across global trade partners.

---

## Dashboard Features

### War Impact Score
- Displays the overall economic impact score on India using a gauge chart.

### Price Index by Month
Tracks monthly price fluctuations for:
- Crude Oil
- Fertilizer
- Wheat

### Import from Russia and Ukraine
- Quarterly and monthly comparison of import values from both countries.

### Inflation and GDP Rate Analysis
Sector-wise comparison of:
- Inflation Rate
- GDP Growth Rate

### Trade Balance and Exchange Rate by Region
Analyzes:
- Trade Volume
- Exchange Rate Trends
Across:
- East
- South
- West
- North

### Trade Value by Trade Partner
Compares India's trade value with:
- EU
- Middle East
- Russia
- Ukraine
- USA

## Key Insights
- Crude oil and fertilizer prices increased significantly during escalation and sanctions phases.
- Imports from Russia increased compared to Ukraine during later phases of the war.
- Inflation rates rose across major sectors, especially Energy and Finance.
- Trade patterns shifted due to geopolitical and supply chain disruptions.
